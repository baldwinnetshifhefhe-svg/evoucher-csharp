Cashless agricultural support that reaches the right producers

The e-Voucher Module lets the Department of Agriculture distribute subsidised inputs to producers through secure digital vouchers — issued by SMS, redeemed at agro-dealers with a one-time PIN, and tracked end-to-end.

How the e-Voucher works

From selecting a producer to proof of redemption.

1
Select & verify

Government screens producers from the Farmer Register and checks RICA.

2
Issue voucher

A subsidy package is assigned and a unique voucher is sent by SMS.

3
Redeem with OTP

The producer redeems inputs at an agro-dealer using a one-time PIN.

4
Pay & report

Dealers invoice, payments clear, and everything is audited & reported.
